Furthermore, the page lists terminations that happened due to a breach of the terms of the grants programs. Additionally, teams might have decided to stop working on the grantâ€”though not necessarily on the project itselfâ€”for various reasons, which is not reflected on this sheet.

The badge can also never be used as a general endorsement for a team. Violations to this policy can be reported [here](mailto:grants@web3.foundation).
:::
